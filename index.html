Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about calculating and completing missed prayers and fasts according to Islamic guidance.

How accurate are the prayer and fast calculations? +

Our calculations are based on established Islamic jurisprudence and consider the age of responsibility (Bulugh), valid exemption periods for women during menstruation, and other circumstances recognized in Islamic law. The system accounts for the standard five daily prayers (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha) and Ramadan fasting obligations from the time you became responsible for these acts of worship.

Is my personal information kept private and secure? +

Absolutely. We take your privacy very seriously. All personal information including your date of birth, gender, and menstruation cycle data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information with third parties, and you have full control over your data. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

Why do I need to provide menstruation cycle information? +

According to Islamic jurisprudence, women are exempt from performing prayers and fasting during menstruation. To provide accurate calculations, we need to subtract these exemption periods from the total count. This information is used solely for calculation purposes and is kept completely private. This ensures that your final count reflects only the prayers and fasts that you were actually required to perform.
